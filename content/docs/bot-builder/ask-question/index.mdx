Boost customer engagement and increase lead conversions by asking the right questions at the right time. With **Hellofy**, you can create interactive conversations that feel personal and purposeful.

## 💡 Why Use Ask Question Cards?

Use these cards whenever you need to **capture user input** during a chat. Whether it’s collecting contact details, preferences, or choices—these cards help you drive deeper interactions and qualify leads effectively.

## 🧩 Available Question Types in Hellofy
- **Ask Text** – Collect open-ended responses
- **Ask Email** – Capture verified email addresses
- **Ask List** – Let users choose from a dropdown-style list
- **Ask Button** – Present clickable options for quick replies

🎯 _Use these powerful input cards to guide conversations, gather insights, and convert interest into action—right from your bot._
