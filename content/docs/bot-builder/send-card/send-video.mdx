Engage your customers with dynamic content using the **Send Video Card** in **Hellofy.**\
Whether it’s a product demo, tutorial, or welcome message—sharing videos has never been easier within your chatbot.

## 👥 Who Can Use This Card?

- Available to all **Hellofy** users with permissions to build, deploy, or manage chatbots
- Included in **all paid Hellofy plans**

## 📼 What Does the Send Video Card Do?

Use this card to send a **video file** directly to your customers during a conversation.

**Requirements:** - ✅ Supported formats: MP4 and 3GPP - 📏 Max file size: 16 MB - 📝 You can also add a caption to describe the video or add a message alongside it

## ✍️ Add a Caption

Enhance your video with a short message in the **Caption** field—use it to explain what the video covers, highlight key points, or include a call to action for better engagement.

## 🔁 Manage Your Video Card

Click the **three-dot menu** on the card to:

- ✏️ Edit the video or caption
- 🗑️ Delete the card
- 📄 Duplicate the card for reuse in other flows
