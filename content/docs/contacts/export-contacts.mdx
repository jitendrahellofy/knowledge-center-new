---
title: "Export Contacts"
---

Easily export your contact records from your Hellofy account and download them directly to your device. You can also view and manage your export history anytime.

## How to Export Contacts:

- In your Hellofy account, go to the Contacts tab.
- Click on the three-dot menu at the top right and select Export.
- Once the export is complete, the file will be downloaded to your device.

You can access past exports anytime from the Export History section.
