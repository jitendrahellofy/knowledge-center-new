## Edit Contact

Click on any contact to update details like name, email, phone number, tags, and custom fields. This is especially helpful when you have contacts with similar names.

## Delete Contact Individually

To delete a single contact, open the contact card, click on the three-dot menu, and choose Delete.

## Bulk Delete Contacts

Select multiple contacts by ticking the checkboxes beside their names, then click Delete from the top action bar.
