### üßæ Template Mapping and Personalization

After selecting a group:

#### Step 3: Choose an Approved Template
Select a pre-approved WhatsApp message template from the dropdown.

<DefaultVector />

#### Step 4: Map Template Variables

For each variable `(e.g., {{Name}}, {{Phone}})`, choose:

- **Fetch from contact:** Auto-populates from CRM fields
- **Enter my own:** Manually input a static value

<DefaultVector />

#### Step 5: Schedule Your Campaign
Choose one:

- **Send Immediately:** Campaign sends right after confirmation.
- **Schedule for Later:** Set a future date/time (up to 3 months ahead).

<DefaultVector />

#### üí≥ Note on Credit Blocking:
When a campaign is scheduled, the estimated message cost is **blocked from your wallet.** This amount is deducted at the time of scheduling.
